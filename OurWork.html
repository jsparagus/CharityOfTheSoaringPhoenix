Humanitarian Response

Since 2021, CSP has helped the Philippines with development and disaster relief. The group has built solid working relationships with local communities and neighborhood non-governmental organizations around the nation over the years. Within the nation, CSP responds to both big and minor calamities, including typhoons, earthquakes, volcanic eruptions, and displacements. For our reactions, CSP closely collaborates with our local partners to create a thorough risk assessment that takes into account the particular requirements of the impacted community.


Health and Wash

The CSP addresses non-communicable diseases (NCDs) in internally displaced communities, offers comprehensive quality sexual and reproductive health and rights (SRHR) services to women and girls of reproductive age, and builds public health sector capabilities in SRHR health care and NCD management, including in emergencies. In Mindanao, CSP conducts onsite health visits to help community residents impacted by the bloody conflict and frequent evictions receive primary healthcare consultations and nutrition checks. For at-risk groups to get health, nutrition, and psychosocial support, CSP also offers referrals and logistical support.


Integrated Risk Management


CSP helps local advocates and at-risk communities in the Philippines to increase their ability to withstand calamities. As a result, CSP is integrating catastrophe risk reduction, adaptation to climate change, and ecosystem restoration across all of our initiatives. The resilience method used by CSP functions both as a temporary component of our programmatic thinking and as a response to shocks and pressures. It also goes hand in hand with including inclusive governance and gender equality as key pillars of the journey out of poverty and toward a life of dignity.



CSP creates opportunities for knowledge exchange among and between many key development and humanitarian actors. Within CSP and in collaboration with other organizations, CSP takes part in regional and global research.


CSP makes use of the knowledge and creativity gained from in-depth research, notably on resilience, gender, and humanitarian aid. The Resilience and Innovation Learning Hub Philippines, or RILHUB, is a platform for learning and reflection as well as a source of information in innovations, ecosystem management and restoration, and disaster risk reduction (DRR), as well as CCA and DRR.
